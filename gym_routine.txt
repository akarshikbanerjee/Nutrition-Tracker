⭐ Your 4-Day Weekly Program (1 hour each)
Day 1 — Chest + Shoulders (Front/Side) + Triceps
Goal: chest shape, shoulder width, upper-body definition.
Warm-up: 5 min incline treadmill
Workout (45–50 min):
Dumbbell Bench Press — 3×8–10, 25–30 lb each
Incline Dumbbell Press — 3×8, 20–25 lb
Cable Fly or Pec Deck — 2×12–15 (light)
Dumbbell Shoulder Press — 3×8, 20–25 lb
Lateral Raises — 3×12–15, 10–12 lb
Triceps Rope Pushdown — 2×12, light–moderate
Cool-down: 3 min stretch


⭐ Day 2 — Back + Traps + Biceps
Goal: V-shape, bigger arms, stronger posture, traps included.
Warm-up: 5 min rower
Workout (45–50 min):
Lat Pulldown — 3×8–10
Machine Row or Dumbbell Row — 3×8–10
Face Pulls — 2×12–15 (rear delts + upper traps)
Dumbbell Shrugs — 3×10–12, 40–50 lb each
Biceps Dumbbell Curls — 2×10, 15–20 lb
Hammer Curls — 2×10
Cool-down: 3 min stretch


⭐ Day 3 — Legs + Abs
Goal: calorie burn, athletic look, reduce belly fat by increasing total workload.
Warm-up: 5 min bike
Workout (45–50 min):
Goblet Squat — 3×10, 30–40 lb (safer beginner version than barbell)
Leg Press — 3×10
Hamstring Curl Machine — 3×12
Calf Raises — 2×12–15
Planks — 3×30 seconds
Cable Woodchoppers — 2×12 per side
Cool-down: 3 min stretch


⭐ Day 4 — Shoulders + Arms (Shape Day)
Goal: shoulder shape, chest line, arms definition. Very aesthetic day.
Warm-up: 5 min treadmill
Workout (45–50 min):
Incline Dumbbell Press (light) — 2×12, 20 lb
(for upper chest shape)
Machine Shoulder Press — 3×8–10
Lateral Raises — 3×12–15, 10–12 lb
Reverse Pec Deck — 2×12
Dumbbell Shrugs — 2×12
Triceps Rope Extension — 2×12
EZ-Bar Biceps Curl — 2×10
Cable Chest Press Finisher — 1×15 (light)
Cool-down: 3 min stretch
